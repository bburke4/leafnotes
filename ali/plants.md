# Ali's Front Garden

Zone: 6a
Planted: May 2026 (all new)
Light: mixed sun and shade

A native pollinator garden — 12 of 13 plants are NE natives, with Lily of the Valley as the one heritage exception.

## Plants

- Bee Balm (Monarda) × 1
- Lupine (Lupinus perennis) × 1
- Wild Columbine (Aquilegia canadensis) × 2
- False Indigo (Baptisia australis) × 1
- Jacob's Ladder 'Hurricane' (Polemonium reptans 'Hurricane') × 1
- Lily of the Valley (Convallaria majalis) × 1 — ⚠️ aggressive spreader, toxic
- Milkweed (Asclepias) × 1 — monarch host plant
- Foamflower (Tiarella cordifolia) × 1
- Obedient Plant / False Dragonhead (Physostegia virginiana) × 1 — spreads
- Spiderwort (Tradescantia virginiana) × 1
- Golden Alexander (Zizia aurea) × 1 — black swallowtail host
- Bloodroot (Sanguinaria canadensis) × 1 — spring ephemeral, foliage goes dormant in summer
